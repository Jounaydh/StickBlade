# Inkronin Character Brief

## Goal

Create a demo-ready main character for StickBlade: a stylized ninja/ronin with a black silhouette, cyan markings, and a katana. The design should look premium in screenshots but stay efficient enough for Android and iOS later.

## Visual Direction

- Small masked ninja body with round shadow face.
- Bright cyan vertical eyes.
- Messy spiked black hair.
- Cyan headband with two trailing cloth strips.
- Layered black robe, scarf, torn cloth edges, loose pants, wrist wraps, ankle wraps.
- Cyan sash and thin cyan trim lines.
- Circular cyan moon mark on the back.
- Katana with black blade/body and cyan edge/handle accents.

## Mobile Budget Target

- One skinned mesh for the final character.
- 2,000 to 6,000 triangles for gameplay.
- One 1024 texture atlas, or flat materials only.
- One humanoid rig.
- 15 to 30 bones.
- No cloth simulation for gameplay.
- No real-time hair physics for gameplay.

## Required Animations

- Idle combat stance.
- Walk.
- Run.
- Ninja dash.
- Sword draw.
- Light slash 1.
- Light slash 2.
- Parry/deflect.
- Stagger.
- Deathblow.

## Current Prototype Asset

The generated Unity prefab is a temporary demo model, not the final production character. It is useful for testing silhouette, colors, movement, camera, and combat feel before building or commissioning the real rigged model.
